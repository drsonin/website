### Why Professional Teeth Cleaning Matters

Even the most dedicated daily oral hygiene routine cannot remove all plaque and tartar buildup. Over time, minerals in saliva cause plaque to harden into tartar — a stubborn deposit that can only be safely removed by a dental professional. Left untreated, tartar accumulation leads to gum inflammation, periodontitis, and even tooth loss.

Professional teeth cleaning, also known as prophylaxis or scale and polish, removes these deposits thoroughly and safely. It also gives your dentist the opportunity to detect early signs of cavities, gum disease, or other oral health issues before they become costly and painful problems.

### What Happens During a Professional Cleaning?

At Sonin Hambaravi clinic, every professional cleaning follows a careful, step-by-step process designed to be both effective and comfortable:

- **Scaling** — A dental hygienist or dentist uses specialised ultrasonic and hand instruments to remove tartar and plaque from the surfaces of the teeth and along the gumline.
- **Polishing** — A gentle polishing paste removes surface stains, leaving your teeth smooth and noticeably cleaner.
- **Flossing and rinsing** — Any remaining debris is cleared away, and an antibacterial rinse may be applied.
- **Examination** — Dr Dmitri Sonin reviews the condition of your teeth and gums, discussing any concerns and recommending a personalised care plan if needed.

The entire appointment typically takes between 45 and 60 minutes and is completely painless for most patients.

### How Often Should You Have Your Teeth Cleaned?

Most dental professionals recommend a professional cleaning every six months. However, the ideal frequency depends on your individual oral health. Patients with a history of gum disease, heavy tartar buildup, or certain medical conditions may benefit from more frequent visits — every three to four months.

During your initial consultation, Dmitri Sonin will assess your specific needs and help you establish a cleaning schedule that keeps your mouth healthy year-round.
