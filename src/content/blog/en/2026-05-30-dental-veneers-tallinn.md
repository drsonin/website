### What Are Dental Veneers?

Dental veneers are ultra-thin shells — typically made from porcelain or composite resin — that are bonded directly to the front surface of your teeth. They are custom-crafted to match the natural shape and colour of your smile, while correcting a wide range of aesthetic concerns, including:

- Discolouration or staining that whitening cannot fix
- Chipped or cracked teeth
- Uneven or slightly misaligned teeth
- Gaps between teeth
- Teeth that appear too small or irregularly shaped

The result is a natural-looking, durable smile that feels entirely your own.

### The Veneer Treatment Process at Sonin Hambaravi

Understanding what to expect can make the process feel much less daunting. Here is a typical step-by-step overview of how veneer treatment works at the clinic:

**1. Initial Consultation**
Your journey begins with a thorough consultation, during which Dmitri Sonin evaluates your oral health, discusses your aesthetic goals, and presents the most suitable treatment options. Digital imaging may be used to give you a preview of your potential results.

**2. Tooth Preparation**
A small, minimal amount of enamel is gently removed from the tooth surface to ensure the veneer sits flush and natural-looking. Local anaesthesia is used to keep you comfortable throughout.

**3. Impressions and Design**
Precise impressions of your teeth are taken and sent to a specialist dental laboratory. Each veneer is crafted individually to your specifications, ensuring a seamless fit.

**4. Bonding**
Once your veneers are ready — usually within one to two weeks — they are carefully bonded to your teeth using a strong dental adhesive. Final adjustments are made to ensure a perfect bite and appearance.

**5. Aftercare and Follow-Up**
The team at Sonin Hambaravi provides detailed aftercare guidance and schedules follow-up appointments to monitor your results and ensure lasting satisfaction.

### Porcelain vs Composite Veneers: Which Is Right for You?

One of the most common questions patients ask is whether to choose porcelain or composite veneers. Both have their advantages:

- **Porcelain veneers** are highly durable, stain-resistant, and deliver the most lifelike aesthetic. They typically last 10–15 years or longer with proper care.
- **Composite veneers** can often be completed in a single visit, involve less tooth preparation, and are more affordable — making them a great option for patients seeking a quicker transformation.

During your consultation, Dr Dmitri Sonin will recommend the most appropriate material based on your specific dental needs, lifestyle, and budget.

### How to Care for Your Veneers

With the right care, veneers can maintain their brilliance for many years. Key aftercare tips include:

- Brushing twice daily with a non-abrasive toothpaste
- Flossing regularly to protect the gum line
- Avoiding excessive force on the veneers (e.g., biting nails or hard objects)
- Attending regular check-ups at your dental clinic
- Wearing a nightguard if you grind your teeth
