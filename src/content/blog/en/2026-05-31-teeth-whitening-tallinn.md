### Why Choose Professional Teeth Whitening?

Many patients try over-the-counter whitening strips or home remedies before visiting a dental clinic. While these products can offer modest results, they rarely match the effectiveness or safety of a professional treatment. Store-bought solutions contain lower concentrations of active whitening agents and are not tailored to your individual tooth structure or existing dental work.

Professional teeth whitening performed by a qualified dentist ensures that:

- The correct concentration of whitening gel is used for your specific needs
- Your gums and soft tissues are fully protected throughout the procedure
- Existing restorations such as crowns or veneers are taken into account
- Results are noticeably brighter — often by several shades in a single session

### What to Expect at Sonin Hambaravi

At our Tallinn clinic, Dr Dmitri Sonin begins every whitening consultation with a thorough examination of your oral health. This step is essential. Whitening treatment works best on healthy teeth and gums, so any underlying issues such as cavities or gum sensitivity are addressed first.

Once your teeth are cleared for treatment, you can choose between two approaches depending on your preferences and lifestyle:

**In-clinic whitening** delivers the fastest and most dramatic results. A professional-grade bleaching agent is applied directly to your teeth and activated using a special light. The entire appointment typically takes around 60 to 90 minutes, and most patients leave the clinic with a smile that is significantly whiter.

**Take-home whitening trays** offer a more gradual approach. Custom-fitted trays are made from precise impressions of your teeth, ensuring even gel distribution and maximum comfort. You apply the gel yourself at home over one to two weeks, following the specific guidance provided by our team.

Both methods are safe, effective, and produce long-lasting results when combined with good oral hygiene habits.

### Who Is a Good Candidate for Teeth Whitening?

Teeth whitening is suitable for most adults who have healthy teeth and realistic expectations. It is particularly effective for removing stains caused by coffee, tea, red wine, and smoking — the most common culprits behind discolouration.

However, whitening may not be appropriate in every case. Dmitri Sonin advises patients that certain types of discolouration — such as intrinsic staining caused by medications or dental trauma — may respond better to alternative cosmetic treatments like veneers or bonding. This is why an individual consultation is always the starting point at Sonin Hambaravi.

### How Long Do Results Last?

The longevity of your whitening results depends on your diet and lifestyle habits. Most patients enjoy noticeably whiter teeth for one to three years after treatment. Avoiding staining foods and drinks, maintaining regular brushing and flossing, and attending routine dental check-ups all help to preserve your results.

Occasional top-up treatments at the clinic or using your custom take-home trays can also extend the brightness of your smile over time.
