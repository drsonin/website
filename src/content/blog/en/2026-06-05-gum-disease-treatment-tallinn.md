### What Is Gum Disease and Why Does It Matter?

Gum disease, also known as periodontal disease, is a bacterial infection that affects the tissues surrounding and supporting your teeth. It typically progresses in two stages:

- **Gingivitis** — the early, reversible stage characterised by red, swollen, or bleeding gums
- **Periodontitis** — the more advanced stage where infection spreads below the gum line, damaging bone and connective tissue

Left untreated, periodontitis can lead to tooth loss and has been linked to broader systemic health issues, including cardiovascular disease and diabetes. Early diagnosis and treatment are therefore essential — not just for your oral health, but for your overall wellbeing.

### Common Signs You May Need Gum Disease Treatment

Many patients are unaware they have gum disease until a dental professional identifies it during a check-up. However, there are warning signs worth paying attention to:

- Gums that bleed when brushing or flossing
- Persistent bad breath that doesn't resolve with regular brushing
- Gums that appear to be pulling away from your teeth
- Increased tooth sensitivity or loose teeth
- Visible pus between the teeth and gums

### How Gum Disease Is Treated at Sonin Hambaravi

At Sonin Hambaravi clinic, gum disease treatment is approached with precision and care. The exact treatment plan depends on the severity of your condition, but typically includes one or more of the following:

**Professional Dental Cleaning and Scaling**
For early-stage gingivitis, a thorough professional cleaning — including the removal of plaque and tartar above and below the gum line — is often sufficient to reverse symptoms and restore gum health.

**Scaling and Root Planing**
For more advanced periodontitis, a deep-cleaning procedure called scaling and root planing is recommended. This involves carefully removing bacterial deposits from the root surfaces of the teeth and smoothing them to prevent future bacterial buildup. The procedure is carried out under local anaesthesia to ensure patient comfort.

**Antibacterial Therapy**
In some cases, antimicrobial rinses or locally applied antibiotics are used alongside cleaning procedures to eliminate stubborn bacterial infections more effectively.

**Surgical Periodontal Treatment**
In severe cases where non-surgical approaches are insufficient, surgical intervention may be necessary. Dr Dmitri Sonin will discuss all available options transparently, ensuring patients understand each step of their treatment journey.

**Ongoing Maintenance and Prevention**
Treating gum disease is only the beginning. Long-term success depends on consistent follow-up appointments and a strong home care routine. The team at Sonin Hambaravi provides personalised oral hygiene guidance to help patients maintain healthy gums between visits.
