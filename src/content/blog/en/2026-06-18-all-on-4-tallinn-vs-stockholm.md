## All-on-4 Tallinn vs Stockholm Price Comparison – Insights from Dr Dmitri Sonin

If you are considering full-arch dental implants and wondering whether it makes financial sense to travel to Estonia for treatment, you are not alone. Every year, hundreds of Scandinavian patients make the short trip to Tallinn to access world-class dental care at a fraction of the price they would pay at home. Below, Dr Dmitri Sonin breaks down the real costs involved and explains why Tallinn has become one of Europe's most popular destinations for All-on-4 treatment.

### What Is All-on-4 and Why Is It So Popular?

All-on-4 is a full-arch dental restoration technique that uses just four strategically placed implants to support a complete set of fixed teeth. It is a life-changing solution for patients who have lost most or all of their teeth, offering a permanent, natural-looking smile in a surprisingly short timeframe. Because fewer implants are required compared to traditional full-arch restorations, the procedure is faster, less invasive, and more cost-effective — without compromising on quality or longevity.

### All-on-4 Prices in Stockholm

In Stockholm, the cost of All-on-4 treatment for a single jaw typically ranges between **€18,000 and €28,000**. For patients requiring both upper and lower arches, the total investment can easily reach **€35,000–€50,000** or more. These prices reflect Sweden's high cost of living, elevated staff wages, and premium clinic overheads. Swedish dental insurance rarely covers implant-based treatments in full, leaving most patients to fund a significant portion themselves.

### All-on-4 Prices in Tallinn

At Sonin Hambaravi clinic in Tallinn, All-on-4 treatment for a single jaw is available from approximately **€7,000 to €11,000**, depending on the chosen implant system and prosthetic material. Full-mouth treatment for both arches typically falls in the range of **€13,000–€20,000** — representing a saving of up to **60% compared to Stockholm prices**.

Dmitri Sonin emphasises that lower cost does not mean lower standards. The clinic uses leading implant systems from trusted manufacturers, applies the same clinical protocols used across top European practices, and works with highly trained specialists who hold internationally recognised qualifications.

### What Is Included in the Price?

Transparent pricing is a cornerstone of the Sonin Hambaravi approach. A typical All-on-4 package in Tallinn includes:

- Initial consultation and digital X-rays or 3D CBCT scan
- Treatment planning and implant placement surgery
- Temporary prosthesis fitted on the day of surgery
- Final fixed bridge (acrylic, zirconia, or hybrid ceramic options available)
- Follow-up appointments during the healing period

Patients are always advised to confirm exactly what is included before committing to treatment anywhere, as some clinics advertise low headline prices that exclude essential components such as the final bridge or post-operative care.

### The Travel Factor: Is It Worth It?

For patients travelling from Stockholm, Tallinn is remarkably accessible. The Viking Line or Tallink ferry takes approximately 16 hours, making it a comfortable overnight crossing. Alternatively, direct flights from Stockholm Arlanda to Tallinn take just over one hour. Budget airlines operate this route regularly, with fares often available from as little as €40–€80 return.

When you factor in travel and accommodation costs, most Stockholm patients still save **€10,000 or more** on full-mouth All-on-4 treatment — even after accounting for two or three visits to Tallinn over the course of the treatment process.
