### What Is Professional Teeth Cleaning?

Professional teeth cleaning, also known as dental prophylaxis or hygiene treatment, is a thorough procedure performed by a dentist or dental hygienist. During the appointment, built-up plaque and tartar (calculus) are removed from all surfaces of the teeth, including areas that a toothbrush simply cannot reach.

The process typically includes:

- **Scaling** — removing hardened tartar above and below the gumline
- **Polishing** — buffing away surface stains and smoothing the enamel
- **Flossing and irrigation** — clearing debris from between the teeth
- **Fluoride treatment** — strengthening enamel and reducing sensitivity (when indicated)

Most patients are advised to have a professional cleaning every six months, though those with gum disease or a higher risk of cavities may benefit from more frequent visits.

### Benefits of Regular Professional Cleaning

Scheduling routine hygiene appointments delivers real, measurable benefits:

- **Prevents gum disease** — Gingivitis and periodontitis are among the most common dental conditions in adults. Regular removal of bacterial deposits keeps gums healthy and firm.
- **Reduces the risk of cavities** — Plaque produces acids that erode enamel. Professional cleaning removes this threat before damage can occur.
- **Freshens breath** — Persistent bad breath is often caused by bacterial build-up that brushing alone cannot address.
- **Brightens your smile** — Surface stains are polished away, leaving teeth noticeably cleaner and whiter.
- **Enables early detection** — During your cleaning, **Dr Dmitri Sonin** or a member of the clinical team will screen for early signs of decay, oral lesions, or other concerns — catching issues before they become costly.
