### The Implant Process at Sonin Hambaravi

Dmitri Sonin and his team take a thorough, patient-centred approach to implant treatment. A typical implant journey includes:

- **Initial consultation and 3D imaging** — to assess bone density and plan exact implant positioning
- **Implant placement** — a minor surgical procedure performed under local anaesthesia
- **Healing period** — usually 3–6 months for osseointegration (the implant fusing with the jawbone)
- **Crown fitting** — a natural-looking ceramic crown is attached to complete your new tooth

Many Finnish patients plan their treatment across two or three short visits, combining their dental appointments with a pleasant stay in Tallinn's beautiful Old Town.
