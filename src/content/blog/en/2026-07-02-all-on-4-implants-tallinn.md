### What Are All-on-4 Dental Implants?

All-on-4 is a full-arch dental implant solution designed to replace an entire row of missing teeth — upper, lower, or both — using only four implants per arch. Two implants are placed vertically at the front of the jaw, while two are angled at the back to maximise contact with available bone. This clever angulation means that even patients with significant bone loss can often avoid the need for bone grafting, which traditionally added time, cost, and recovery to the implant process.

The result is a full fixed bridge that looks, feels, and functions like natural teeth — secured permanently in your mouth and never removed like a denture.

### The All-on-4 Process at Sonin Dental Clinic

Dr Dmitri Sonin follows a structured, patient-centred approach to every All-on-4 case. The process typically begins with a comprehensive consultation, including a detailed 3D CBCT scan to assess bone density, jaw structure, and the precise positioning of nerves and sinuses. This digital planning phase is essential to achieving predictable, safe results.

On the day of surgery, implants are placed under local anaesthesia, with sedation options available for anxious patients. In most cases, a temporary fixed bridge is attached to the implants on the same day — meaning patients leave the clinic with a functional smile within hours of their procedure. After a healing period of several months, the final permanent prosthesis is fitted, crafted from high-grade materials for durability and aesthetics.

Throughout each stage, the team at Sonin Dental Clinic provides clear communication, aftercare guidance, and follow-up appointments to ensure a smooth recovery and excellent long-term results.

### Who Is a Good Candidate?

All-on-4 is suitable for a wide range of patients, including those who:

- Have lost most or all teeth in one or both arches
- Wear removable dentures and find them uncomfortable or unstable
- Have been told they lack sufficient bone for traditional implants
- Are in good general health and are non-smokers, or willing to quit before treatment

A thorough assessment at Sonin Dental Clinic will confirm whether All-on-4 is the right solution, or whether an alternative approach — such as All-on-6 or individual implants — might better serve your needs.
