## All-on-4 Dental Implants in Tallinn: A Complete Guide by Dr Dmitri Sonin

### What Are All-on-4 Dental Implants?

All-on-4 is a full-arch restoration technique that uses just four strategically placed implants to support an entire set of fixed replacement teeth. Unlike traditional implants, which require one implant per missing tooth, the All-on-4 method maximises the use of available bone — including areas of denser bone at the front of the jaw — to anchor a complete prosthetic arch securely in place.

The result is a stable, natural-looking smile that functions just like real teeth. Patients can eat, speak, and smile without the worry of dentures shifting or falling out.

### Who Is a Good Candidate for All-on-4?

All-on-4 implants are ideal for patients who:

- Have lost most or all of their teeth in one or both jaws
- Struggle with ill-fitting or uncomfortable dentures
- Have experienced significant bone loss that might rule out conventional implants
- Want a permanent, low-maintenance solution

One of the key advantages of the All-on-4 approach is that it often works even for patients with reduced bone density, thanks to the angled placement of the rear implants. During your consultation at Sonin Dental Clinic, a thorough assessment — including 3D imaging — will determine whether this treatment is right for you.

### The All-on-4 Treatment Process at Sonin Dental Clinic

Dr Dmitri Sonin follows a meticulous, patient-centred process to ensure the best possible outcomes:

**1. Initial Consultation and Planning**
Your journey begins with a comprehensive examination of your oral health, bone structure, and dental history. Advanced digital scanning allows for precise implant planning tailored to your unique anatomy.

**2. Implant Placement**
The four implants are placed under local anaesthesia, often with sedation options available for patients who feel anxious. In many cases, a temporary fixed arch can be fitted on the same day — giving you functional teeth immediately after the procedure.

**3. Healing and Osseointegration**
Over the following months, the implants fuse with the jawbone in a process called osseointegration. This biological bond is what makes the final restoration so stable and durable.

**4. Final Restoration**
Once healing is complete, your permanent prosthetic arch is fitted. Crafted from high-quality materials, it is designed to look natural and withstand the demands of everyday life.
