### What Is the All-on-4 Implant Technique?

All-on-4 is a fixed full-arch restoration supported by just four strategically placed dental implants. Unlike traditional implants that require one implant per missing tooth, this technique uses four anchor points to support a complete set of prosthetic teeth on the upper or lower jaw — sometimes both.

The benefits are significant:

- **Immediate results** — many patients leave with a temporary fixed bridge the same day
- **Bone preservation** — implants stimulate the jawbone and prevent further deterioration
- **Stability** — no slipping or adhesives, unlike removable dentures
- **Long-term value** — with proper care, the implants can last a lifetime

### All-on-4 Price in Tallinn: What to Expect

One of the most common questions patients ask is: *"How much does All-on-4 cost in Tallinn?"* Pricing depends on several factors, but Tallinn consistently offers outstanding value compared to Western Europe or Scandinavia.

At Sonin Hambaravi clinic, the typical price range for a **full-arch All-on-4 treatment** is:

- **One jaw (upper or lower):** from €6,500 to €9,500
- **Both jaws (full mouth):** from €13,000 to €18,000

These figures include the implant placement surgery, the temporary prosthesis placed on the day of treatment, and the final permanent bridge fitted after healing. Exact costs are confirmed only after a personal consultation and a 3D cone-beam CT scan of your jaw.

### Factors That Affect the Final Cost

Several clinical and material factors influence the total price:

**Bone quality and density.** Patients with significant bone loss may require bone grafting prior to implant placement, which adds to the overall treatment cost and timeline.

**Choice of prosthetic material.** The final bridge can be made from high-quality acrylic, zirconia, or hybrid ceramic. Zirconia is the premium option — more durable and natural-looking — and is reflected in the price.

**Implant brand.** Sonin Hambaravi uses implants from internationally certified manufacturers, ensuring reliability and long-term performance.

**Number of procedures needed.** Some patients need extractions, sinus lifts, or other preparatory work before implants can be placed.
