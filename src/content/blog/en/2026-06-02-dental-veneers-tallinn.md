### What Are Dental Veneers?

Dental veneers are ultra-thin shells — typically made from porcelain or composite resin — that are bonded to the front surface of your teeth. They are custom-crafted to match your natural tooth colour, shape, and size, creating a seamlessly beautiful result that looks completely natural.

Veneers are an ideal solution for:

- Discoloured or stained teeth that don't respond to whitening
- Chipped or cracked teeth
- Gaps between teeth
- Slightly misaligned or uneven teeth
- Worn-down enamel

Because veneers address multiple cosmetic concerns at once, they are one of the most versatile treatments in modern cosmetic dentistry.

### The Veneer Treatment Process at Sonin Hambaravi

Understanding what to expect helps patients feel comfortable and confident before sitting in the chair. Here is a straightforward overview of how the process works at our clinic:

**1. Initial Consultation**
Your journey begins with a thorough consultation. Dmitri Sonin will assess your oral health, discuss your aesthetic goals, and recommend the most suitable type of veneer for your needs. Digital photographs and impressions may be taken at this stage.

**2. Tooth Preparation**
A small, minimal amount of enamel is gently removed from the tooth surface to ensure the veneer fits flush and naturally. This step is carefully performed to preserve as much of your natural tooth structure as possible.

**3. Impressions and Shade Selection**
Precise impressions of your teeth are sent to a dental laboratory where your custom veneers are crafted. You will also choose the exact shade to complement your complexion and personal preference.

**4. Temporary Veneers**
In most cases, temporary veneers are placed while your permanent ones are being made, so you are never without a complete smile.

**5. Fitting and Bonding**
Once your final veneers arrive, Dr Dmitri Sonin carefully bonds them to your teeth using a strong dental adhesive and checks your bite for comfort and precision. Any final adjustments are made to ensure a perfect fit.

### How Long Do Veneers Last?

With proper care, porcelain veneers can last 10 to 15 years or even longer. Maintaining good oral hygiene — brushing twice daily, flossing, and attending regular check-ups — will keep your veneers looking their best. Avoiding habits such as nail biting, chewing on hard objects, or grinding your teeth will also extend their lifespan significantly.

---

### Are Veneers Right for You?

Veneers are suitable for most healthy adults looking to improve the appearance of their smile. However, they may not be recommended if you have active gum disease, significant tooth decay, or severe bruxism (teeth grinding) that is unmanaged. During your consultation, we will thoroughly evaluate your situation and discuss all available options to find the best path forward for your individual needs.
