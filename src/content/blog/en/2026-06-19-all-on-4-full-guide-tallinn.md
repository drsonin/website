### What Are All-on-4 Dental Implants?

All-on-4 is a full-arch restoration method that uses just **four titanium implants** to support an entire set of fixed teeth — either for the upper jaw, lower jaw, or both. Unlike traditional implants, which may require one implant per missing tooth, All-on-4 is designed for efficiency and reliability.

The four implants are placed at strategic angles to maximise contact with the available bone. This often means patients who have experienced bone loss — a common consequence of long-term tooth loss — are still **excellent candidates** without needing a bone graft first.

---

### Who Is a Good Candidate?

All-on-4 is typically recommended for patients who:

- Are missing all or most teeth in one or both jaws
- Have been told they lack sufficient bone for standard implants
- Currently use removable dentures and want a permanent alternative
- Are in good general health with no uncontrolled systemic conditions

During your consultation at Sonin Hambaravi, **Dmitri Sonin** will assess your oral health, review 3D imaging of your jawbone, and create a treatment plan tailored specifically to you.

### The All-on-4 Procedure: Step by Step

**1. Consultation and 3D Imaging**
Your journey begins with a thorough examination, including digital X-rays or a CBCT scan. This helps the team visualise your bone structure and plan precise implant placement.

**2. Implant Placement**
Under local anaesthesia (sedation is also available), four implants are inserted into the jawbone. Any remaining failing teeth are extracted at this stage.

**3. Temporary Prosthesis**
In most cases, a temporary fixed bridge is attached on the same day — meaning you leave the clinic with teeth already in place.

**4. Healing Period**
Over the next three to six months, the implants integrate with the bone through a process called osseointegration. During this time you follow a soft-food diet and attend follow-up appointments.

**5. Permanent Prosthesis**
Once healing is confirmed, your custom-made permanent bridge — crafted from high-quality porcelain or zirconia — is fitted for a natural, long-lasting result.
