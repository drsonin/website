### What Are All-on-4 Dental Implants?

All-on-4 is a full-arch dental implant technique that supports an entire set of prosthetic teeth using just four strategically placed titanium implants. Unlike traditional implants, which may require one implant per missing tooth, the All-on-4 method uses two straight implants at the front of the jaw and two angled implants at the back. This angulation maximises contact with available bone, often eliminating the need for bone grafting — a major advantage for patients who have experienced bone loss due to prolonged tooth absence.

The result is a fixed, non-removable arch of teeth that looks, feels, and functions like natural dentition. Patients typically leave the clinic with a temporary set of teeth on the very same day as surgery.

### Who Is a Good Candidate for All-on-4?

All-on-4 implants are suitable for a broad range of patients, including those who:

- Are missing all or most teeth in one or both arches
- Have been told they lack sufficient bone for conventional implants
- Currently wear uncomfortable or ill-fitting dentures
- Are looking for a permanent, fixed solution rather than a removable appliance

A thorough consultation and imaging assessment at Sonin Dental Clinic will confirm whether All-on-4 is the right pathway for you. Certain medical conditions, such as uncontrolled diabetes or active gum disease, may need to be addressed before treatment can proceed safely.

### The All-on-4 Treatment Process

The journey typically unfolds across several well-defined stages:

1. **Initial consultation and 3D scanning** — Detailed imaging allows Dmitri Sonin to assess bone structure and plan implant positions with precision.
2. **Treatment planning and temporary prosthesis design** — A temporary arch is prepared in advance so it can be fitted on surgery day.
3. **Surgical placement** — The four implants are inserted under local anaesthesia or sedation, and the temporary prosthesis is attached the same day.
4. **Healing and osseointegration** — Over the following three to six months, the implants fuse with the jawbone.
5. **Final prosthesis fitting** — Once integration is confirmed, the permanent, high-quality fixed arch is placed.

### Long-Term Outcomes and Maintenance

With proper care, All-on-4 implants can last for decades. Patients are advised to maintain thorough oral hygiene using an interdental brush and water flosser, attend regular check-ups, and avoid habits such as smoking that can compromise implant longevity. The fixed arch is not removed for cleaning, which many patients find far more convenient than living with traditional dentures.
