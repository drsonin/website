### Why Swedish Patients Choose Tallinn for Dental Implants

Sweden offers world-class dental care, but it comes at a steep price. A single dental implant in Sweden can cost anywhere between 25,000 and 40,000 SEK. In Tallinn, the same high-quality implant procedure typically costs between 6,000 and 12,000 SEK equivalent — a saving of up to 70%.

Beyond the cost advantage, Tallinn is remarkably accessible from Sweden. Direct ferries run daily from Stockholm to Tallinn (approximately 16 hours), and flights from Stockholm, Gothenburg, and Malmö take as little as 1.5 hours. Many Swedish patients combine their treatment with a pleasant stay in Tallinn's beautiful Old Town.

### What to Expect During Your Treatment Journey

Most single implant cases can be completed in two visits spaced 3–6 months apart. The first visit involves the implant placement and takes approximately 1–2 days. The second visit, for fitting the final crown, takes 1–2 days. Many patients plan the second trip around tourism or business travel to make the most of their time in Estonia.

For All-on-4 or All-on-6 cases, Dmitri Sonin and his team will often provide temporary teeth on the same day as surgery, so you never leave the clinic without a smile. The final permanent restoration is fitted on your follow-up visit.
