## Full Arch Restoration with 4 Implants in Tallinn — Insights from Dr Dmitri Sonin

Losing all or most of your teeth is a life-changing experience. It affects how you eat, how you speak, and how confident you feel when you smile. Fortunately, modern implant dentistry offers a permanent, natural-looking solution — full arch restoration supported by just four implants. At Sonin Hambaravi dental clinic in Tallinn, this treatment has helped hundreds of patients reclaim their quality of life.

### What Is Full Arch Restoration with 4 Implants?

Full arch restoration on four implants — often referred to by the clinical concept "All-on-4" — is a technique that replaces an entire row of teeth using only four strategically placed titanium implants. These implants act as a stable foundation for a fixed prosthetic arch, meaning you get a complete set of teeth that looks, feels, and functions like your natural dentition.

Unlike traditional dentures, which can slip or require adhesive creams, an implant-supported arch is permanently anchored in your jaw. You brush it like natural teeth, eat whatever you like, and forget you ever had a problem.

### Why Only Four Implants?

It might seem surprising that just four implants can support an entire arch of teeth. The secret lies in placement angle. The two posterior implants are tilted at up to 45 degrees, which allows them to anchor into denser bone and distribute chewing forces evenly across the full arch. This approach also eliminates the need for bone grafting in most cases, making the procedure faster, more affordable, and accessible to a wider range of patients.

Dr Dmitri Sonin has refined this technique over years of clinical practice, combining precision diagnostics with a patient-first philosophy to deliver results that are both functional and aesthetically outstanding.

### Who Is a Good Candidate?

Most adults who have lost all or most of their teeth — whether due to decay, gum disease, or injury — are potential candidates for full arch restoration. The procedure is also suitable for patients who currently wear removable dentures and want a more permanent, comfortable alternative.

During your initial consultation at Sonin Hambaravi, the clinical team carries out a thorough assessment including 3D cone beam CT scans to evaluate bone volume and density. Even patients with some degree of bone loss may still qualify, thanks to the angled implant placement technique.

### The Treatment Process at Sonin Hambaravi in Tallinn

The journey to a full smile typically involves the following stages:

- **Consultation and digital planning** — 3D imaging and a detailed treatment plan tailored to your anatomy
- **Implant placement** — a single surgical session performed under local anaesthesia or sedation for your comfort
- **Temporary arch fitting** — in many cases, a provisional set of teeth is attached the same day
- **Final restoration** — once osseointegration (the process of implants fusing with the bone) is complete, usually within three to six months, the permanent ceramic arch is fitted

Dmitri Sonin and his experienced team guide patients through every stage, ensuring clarity, comfort, and confidence throughout the entire process.
