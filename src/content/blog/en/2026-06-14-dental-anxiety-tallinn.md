### What Is Dental Anxiety and Why Does It Matter?

Dental anxiety ranges from mild nervousness before an appointment to a full phobia that prevents patients from seeking any treatment at all. Common triggers include fear of pain, loss of control, negative past experiences, or simply the unfamiliar sounds and smells of a dental environment.

Left unaddressed, dental anxiety leads to delayed treatment, which in turn causes small problems to become serious — and expensive — ones. Cavities become root canals. Mild gum issues develop into periodontal disease. The cycle of avoidance and worsening oral health can have real consequences for your overall wellbeing.

### Recognising the Signs of Dental Fear

Dental anxiety does not always look the same. Some patients experience it as:

- Difficulty sleeping the night before an appointment
- Feeling physically sick or dizzy in the waiting room
- Crying or feeling overwhelmed when thinking about dental visits
- An overwhelming urge to cancel or reschedule at the last moment
- Tensing up or hyperventilating in the dental chair

If any of these sound familiar, know that seeking help is the right step — and the team at Sonin Hambaravi is ready to support you every step of the way.
