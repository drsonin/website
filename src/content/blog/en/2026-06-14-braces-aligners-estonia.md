### Why Orthodontic Treatment Matters Beyond Aesthetics

Straight teeth aren't just about looking good in photos. Properly aligned teeth are easier to clean, reducing the risk of cavities and gum disease. A corrected bite can also relieve jaw tension, reduce headaches, and improve overall oral function.

Estonia has seen a significant rise in demand for orthodontic treatment in recent years, with both adults and teenagers seeking modern solutions. Advances in dental technology mean you no longer have to choose between effectiveness and comfort.

### Traditional Braces: Reliable and Effective

Fixed metal or ceramic braces remain one of the most proven methods for correcting a wide range of dental misalignments. Metal braces use brackets and archwires to gradually shift teeth into the desired position over time.

**Ceramic braces** offer a more discreet alternative. The brackets are tooth-coloured, making them far less visible than traditional metal versions — a popular choice among adult patients who want effective treatment without a noticeable appearance.

At Sonin Hambaravi, Dr Dmitri Sonin carefully evaluates each patient's bite, jaw structure, and aesthetic goals before recommending any treatment plan. Traditional braces remain the preferred choice for complex cases requiring precise, controlled tooth movement.

### Clear Aligners: The Modern Alternative

Clear aligners have transformed orthodontics over the last decade. These are custom-made, removable plastic trays worn over the teeth that gently guide them into alignment through a series of progressive stages.

**Key benefits of clear aligners include:**

- Nearly invisible appearance during treatment
- Removable for eating, brushing, and flossing
- Smooth, comfortable material with no sharp brackets or wires
- Fewer emergency dental visits compared to fixed braces

Clear aligners work particularly well for mild to moderate alignment issues and are especially popular with adult patients in Tallinn and across Estonia who lead active professional lives. Dmitri Sonin works with leading aligner systems to ensure patients receive accurate, digitally planned treatment with predictable results.

### Which Option Is Right for You?

Choosing between braces and aligners depends on several factors: the complexity of your case, your lifestyle, your age, and your personal preferences. There is no one-size-fits-all answer in orthodontics.

- **Complex bite corrections** often benefit most from traditional fixed braces
- **Mild to moderate crowding or spacing** can frequently be addressed with clear aligners
- **Teenagers and children** may be better suited to braces for greater control
- **Adults seeking discretion** often prefer aligners for daily convenience
